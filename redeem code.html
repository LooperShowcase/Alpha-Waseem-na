looking for easy ways to redeem a code in the epic games store?
Open the Epic Games Launcher. Log in to your Epic Games account.
Click your name in the bottom left corner. Click Redeem Code.
enter the code, and then click Redeem. Note: You should receive a message that the code was successfully redeemed.
